[Intro]

[Verse]
Dust on the keys
Bellows catch the breeze
Driving through the pine
Crossing over the line
Wheels hum a song
Where I belong
The road feels long
But I am strong

[Chorus]
Take me home now
Behind the old plow
Accordion plays for the sky
While the eagles fly
Green hills and blue
Waiting for you
I’m passing through
The morning dew

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, the road is open wide
With the spirit as my guide
Let the music slide
On this countryside

Climbing up the hill
Now my hair is grey
At the end of the day
I’ve found my way
Nothing left to say

Everything I need
